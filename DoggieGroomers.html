Full Grooming

Full grooming consists of the following dog being brushed, bathed, and dried, as well as trimmed or clipped with clean, sanitized brushes and clipper blades. Our groomers brush or comb out mats before the bath, making it easier to lather the dog with shampoo.

Basic Grooming

Basic grooming covers services like ear plucking, paw pad shaving, and nail cutting that will keep your doggo's physical health in check.
